# EP-567

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

## Statement
For `G\in\{Q_3,K_{3,3},H_5\}` and `H` with `m` edges/no isolates, ask whether
\[
R(G,H)\ll m.
\]

## Literature Leads
- `EFRS93` and Erdős (1995, p.177) emphasize this family.
- Bradač-Gishboliner-Sudakov (2023): strong progress for subdivisions of `K_4`;
  in particular linear behavior for broad classes and strong `H_5` partials.
- Problem page (edited January 18, 2026) still marks full statement open.
