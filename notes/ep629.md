# EP-629 partial attempt

## Route
Reduced the target `n(k)` to property-B extremal quantities via the known sandwich
`m(k) <= n(k) <= m(k+1)`.

## What is resolved from background
- Baseline bounds are exponential in `k` with polynomial factors.
- Exact values are known for small `k` (`n(2)=6`, `n(3)=14`).

## Hard point
Pinning exact asymptotics for `n(k)` is at least as hard as sharpening asymptotics for smallest non-property-B families.

## Status
Partially resolved with strong bounds, exact growth still open.
