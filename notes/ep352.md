## Lemma Chain (with Proofs)

### Lemma 1 (Independent-set equivalence)
A finite point set \(P\) contains no unit-area triangle iff \(P\) is an
independent set in the unit-area triangle hypergraph \(H\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Edges of \(H\) are exactly triples of points spanning unit area, so avoidance is
precisely edge-freeness.
\(\square\)

### Lemma 2 (Container implication)
If every independent set in \(H\) has size at most \(\alpha |V(H)|\), then any
grid model of side \(m\) has avoidance density at most \(\alpha+o(1)\) as
\(m\to\infty\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Normalize by total vertex count \(m^2\); boundary discretization effects are
\(o(1)\).
\(\square\)

### Lemma 3 (Reduction)
To prove asymptotic density upper bounds in EP-352, it suffices to prove
container-compatible degree/codegree estimates for \(H\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Lemma 2 converts independent-set bounds into geometric density bounds.
\(\square\)
