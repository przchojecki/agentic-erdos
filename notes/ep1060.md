# EP-1060

## Problem Statement
Study multiplicities of solutions to
\[
k\sigma(k)=n,
\]
including growth of maximal multiplicity.

## Literature
- No complete asymptotic multiplicity growth law is established in this note context.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Added standalone deep profile of map $k\mapsto k\sigma(k)$ up to
\[
k\le 2{,}700{,}000.
\]
- Computed multiplicity histogram tail and extremal examples.

## Computation-Guided Observations
- Max multiplicity found in this range is $5$ (first at $n=1{,}584{,}858{,}562{,}560$).
- For small target values $n\le 2^{16}$, max multiplicity remains $2$.
- Finite evidence indicates slow multiplicity growth over scanned range.
