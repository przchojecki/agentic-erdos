# EP-1061

## Problem Statement
Count unordered pairs $(a,b)$, $a<b$, satisfying
\[
\sigma(a)+\sigma(b)=\sigma(a+b),
\]
and study asymptotic growth in terms of $x$ for pairs with $a+b\le x$.

## Literature
- Existence of a clean asymptotic constant law remains unresolved in this note context.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Added standalone deep counting profile up to
\[
X=44{,}000,
\]
with exact divisor-sum sieve and full unordered-pair scan by sum level.

## Computation-Guided Observations
- Counts at probes:
  - $x=12{,}000$: $18{,}898$,
  - $x=20{,}000$: $35{,}806$,
  - $x=44{,}000$: $95{,}533$.
- Ratio count/$x$ increases over this finite range (about $1.19$ at $x=4000$ to about $2.17$ at $x=44000$).
- Finite growth profile is informative but not asymptotic proof.
