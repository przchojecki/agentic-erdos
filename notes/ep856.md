# EP-856

## Problem Statement
Let $k\ge 3$ and let $f_k(N)$ be the maximum of
$$\sum_{n\in A}\frac1n$$
over all $A\subseteq\{1,\dots,N\}$ containing no $k$-subset whose pairwise least common multiples are all equal. Estimate $f_k(N)$.

## Literature
- Erdos gave upper bounds of order roughly $\log N/\log\log N$ in early work.
- Recent progress (including 2025-era references in project notes) links bounds to sunflower-type capacity phenomena.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Implemented deeper randomized greedy search for admissible sets maximizing harmonic weight.
- Ran separate case grids for $k=3$ and $k=4$ with multiple restarts and larger $N$ than earlier quick scans.

## Computation-Guided Observations
- For $k=3$, observed best harmonic-over-log ratios around $0.47$-$0.54$ in tested ranges.
- For $k=4$, observed best harmonic-over-log ratios near $1.0$ in tested ranges.
- Finite data remains consistent with substantially different growth scales by $k$, but does not establish asymptotic exponents.
