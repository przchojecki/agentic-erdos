## LaTeX Proofs for A => B => C Skeleton

### Proposition BM1 (Dyadic harmonic decomposition)
For \(A\subset\mathbb N\),
\[
\sum_{a\in A,\ a\le X}\frac1a
\asymp \sum_{j\le \log_2 X}\frac{|A\cap (2^{j-1},2^j]|}{2^j}.
\]

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
On each dyadic block, \(1/a\) is between \(2^{-j}\) and \(2^{-j+1}\).
\(\square\)

### Proposition BM2 (Reduction)
If one proves
\[
|A\cap [1,N]|\le N\,\phi_k(N)
\]
for all \(k\)-AP-free \(A\) with explicit \(\phi_k\), then BM1 yields
corresponding upper bounds on harmonic sums, and matching constructions determine
\(f(k)\) scale.

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Insert the density bound into each dyadic block in BM1 and sum.
\(\square\)
