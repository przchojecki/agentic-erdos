of \(\mathbb N\), and the target irrationality statement holds for each
\(\mathcal A_i\), then it holds for all infinite \(A\subset\mathbb N\).

\textit{Proof.}

where any rational \(p/q\) with \(q\le Q_N\) stays at distance
\(\gg Q_N^{-2-\eta}\), then irrationality follows for that class.

\textit{Proof.}
