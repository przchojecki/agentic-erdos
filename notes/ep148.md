### Proposition AY1 (Log-recursion transfer)
If for large \(k\),
\[
a_k \le F(k) \le b_k,\quad
\log a_{k+1}\ge \lambda \log a_k,\quad
\log b_{k+1}\le \lambda \log b_k + o(\log b_k),
\]
then upper/lower iterates propagate with common base \(\lambda\) at first order.

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Iterate the inequalities on \(\log\)-scale and compare growth rates.
\(\square\)

from AY1.
