# EP-530

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

## Statement
Given `N` distinct reals, find largest guaranteed Sidon subset size `\ell(N)`
(up to constants/exact asymptotics).

## What is known from background
Known bounds center on `\ell(N) \asymp N^{1/2}`; sharp universal constants remain
open.
