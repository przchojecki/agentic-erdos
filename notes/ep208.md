that each position in a length-\(t\) block is divisible by some \(q_i\), then
there exists an integer interval of length \(t\) with no squarefree number.

\textit{Proof.}

Solve the congruence system by CRT to enforce prescribed square divisibility at

\[
\textit{Proof.}
