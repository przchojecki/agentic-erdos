# EP-507

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

## Statement
Heilbronn triangle problem in the unit disk: maximize the minimum triangle area
among `n` points, and determine asymptotics/exponents for `\alpha(n)`.

## What is known from background
Current upper/lower exponents are separated; modern improvements narrow upper
exponents but do not close the asymptotic order.
