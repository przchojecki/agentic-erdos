# EP-1017

## Problem Statement
For $K_4$-free graphs with
\[
e(G)\ge \left\lfloor\frac{n^2}{4}\right\rfloor+q,
\]
study guaranteed edge-disjoint triangle packing, i.e. lower bounds for
\[
\nu_3(G).
\]

## Literature
- In the $K_4$-free regime, strong positive results are known for the excess-over-Tur\'an formulation.
- The broad sharpened landscape beyond specific structural regimes remains active.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Kept explicit deterministic construction:
  - start from balanced $K_{a,b}$,
  - add $q$ matching edges inside one side,
  - build $q$ pairwise edge-disjoint triangles explicitly.
- Added standalone deterministic verification script over a large finite grid:
  - even $n\in[20,200]$,
  - many $q$ values per $n$,
  - exact structural checks recorded for each instance.

## Computation-Guided Observations
- All tested constructive instances satisfy the certified target
\[
\nu_3(G)\ge q.
\]
- The construction simultaneously keeps $K_4$-freeness and exact edge excess $q$ over $\lfloor n^2/4\rfloor$ by design.

## Status
- Strong constructive family validated at scale.
- General-form refinements beyond this family remain open.
