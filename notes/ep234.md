### Proposition CO1 (CDF compactness principle)
Any sequence of empirical distribution functions \(F_X(c)\) is relatively compact
in the weak topology if tightness holds.

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
By Prokhorov/Helly selection for probability measures on \(\mathbb R_{\ge0}\).

### Proposition CO2 (Uniqueness reduction)

then the limiting distribution is unique; hence \(F_X(c)\to f(c)\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}

Laplace transform uniqueness identifies probability measures.
\(\square\)
