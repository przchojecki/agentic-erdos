### Proposition CR1 (Scaling sandwich template)
If constants \(c_1,c_2>0\) satisfy
\[
c_1 N^{1/3}\le F(N)\le c_2 N^{1/3}
\]
for large \(N\), then \(F(N)=\Theta(N^{1/3})\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Direct definition of \(\Theta(\cdot)\).
\(\square\)

### Proposition CR2 (Constant extraction reduction)
If one proves
\[
\liminf_{N\to\infty}\frac{F(N)}{N^{1/3}}
\ge c
\quad\text{and}\quad
\limsup_{N\to\infty}\frac{F(N)}{N^{1/3}}
\le c,
\]
then \(F(N)\sim cN^{1/3}\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Matching liminf/limsup imply existence of the limit equal to \(c\).
\(\square\)
