# EP-1013

## Problem Statement
Let $h_3(k)$ be the minimum number of vertices in a triangle-free graph of chromatic number $k$. Determine asymptotics and ratio smoothness (e.g. behavior of $h_3(k+1)/h_3(k)$).

## Literature
- Known theory places $h_3(k)$ on roughly $k^2\log k$ scale.
- Mycielski construction gives explicit recursive upper bounds:
\[
h_3(k+1)\le 2h_3(k)+1.
\]
