### Proposition AS1 (Root-growth equivalence)
The statement \(W(k)^{1/k}\to\infty\) is equivalent to:
for every \(C>1\), \(W(k)>C^k\) for all sufficiently large \(k\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Direct unpacking of divergence of the \(k\)-th root.
\(\square\)

### Proposition AS2 (Reduction to superexponential-in-base lower bounds)
If one proves a lower bound
\[
W(k)\ge \exp\!\bigl(k\,\omega(k)\bigr),\qquad \omega(k)\to\infty,
\]
then \(W(k)^{1/k}\to\infty\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Take \(k\)-th roots:
\[
W(k)^{1/k}\ge e^{\omega(k)}\to\infty.
\]
\(\square\)
