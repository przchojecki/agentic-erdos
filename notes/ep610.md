# EP-610 partial attempt

## Route
Compared the global clique-transversal bound target with known extremal lower-barrier constructions from triangle-free Ramsey behavior.

## What is resolved from background
- Known general bound: `tau(G) <= n - sqrt(2n) + O(1)`.
- A stronger `n - c*sqrt(n log n)` form is compatible with known triangle-free independence lower bounds if one could prove the conjectural transfer from EP-151.

## Hard point
Current methods do not establish the required transfer from triangle-free independent-set guarantees to clique-transversal bounds in arbitrary graphs.

## Status
Partially resolved baseline bounds; stronger asymptotics remain open.
