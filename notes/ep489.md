# EP-489

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

### Source: ep489_partial.md

## Statement
For sparse divisor sets `A` with `|A \cap [1,x]| = o(x^{1/2})`, define
`B = {n >= 1 : a | n for all a in A}` and ask whether
\[
\lim_{x\to\infty}\frac{1}{x}\sum_{b_i < x}(b_{i+1}-b_i)^2
\]
exists (and when it is finite).

## What is resolved from background
For `A = {p^2 : p \text{ prime}}` (so `B` is the squarefree numbers), Erdos
proved existence of the limit.

## What remains open in this note
The general sparse-set statement for arbitrary `A` remains unresolved here.
