### Source: ep489_partial.md

## Statement
For sparse divisor sets `A` with `|A \cap [1,x]| = o(x^{1/2})`, define
`B = {n >= 1 : a | n for all a in A}` and ask whether
\[
\lim_{x\to\infty}\frac{1}{x}\sum_{b_i < x}(b_{i+1}-b_i)^2
\]
exists (and when it is finite).

## What is resolved from background
For `A = {p^2 : p \text{ prime}}` (so `B` is the squarefree numbers), Erdos
proved existence of the limit.

## What remains open in this note
The general sparse-set statement for arbitrary `A` remains unresolved here.

## Added Proof Layer (No New Computation)

### Closed Subcase
For
\[
A=\{p^2:p\ \text{prime}\},
\]
the set \(B\) is the squarefree numbers, and the existence of the second-moment
limit is known by Erdős (as recorded in the background). So this model case is
theorem-level resolved.

### Remaining Open Core

## Deeper Proof Program (A => B => C)

### Target
Establish existence (and finiteness criterion) of
\[
L_A:=\lim_{x\to\infty}\frac1x\sum_{b_i<x}(b_{i+1}-b_i)^2
\]
for broad sparse \(A\).

### Step A: Local Pattern Probabilities
For each fixed \(h\), prove asymptotic frequencies
\[
\mathbb P_x(h\text{ consecutive misses of }B)
\]
exist with uniform error in \(h\le H(x)\), \(H(x)\to\infty\) slowly.

### Step B: Gap Tail Control
Derive a uniform tail estimate
\[
\sum_{g>G} g^2\,\mathbb P_x(\text{gap}=g)\le \varepsilon(G)+o_x(1),\qquad \varepsilon(G)\to0,
\]
which gives tightness of second moments.

### Step C: Moment Convergence
Combine Step A + Step B via truncation:
finite-gap part converges by Step A, tail vanishes by Step B, yielding \(L_A\).

### Concrete Blocking Lemma (Most Critical)
Need a quasi-independence theorem for divisibility constraints induced by \(A\):
for moderate pattern windows, joint exclusion probabilities must factor with
summable correlation defect.

### Method Directions
- Tao-style enveloping sieve majorants/minorants for forbidden-divisor sets.
- Cluster expansion on dependency graph of divisibility events.
- Arithmetic-geometry check for pathological \(A\) with high resonance
  (candidate obstruction families).
