# EP-987 deeper attempt

## Statement
For `A_k = limsup_n |sum_{j<=n} e(k x_j)|`, must `limsup_k A_k = infinity`? Is `A_k=o(k)`
possible?

## Computation in this attempt
Finite-`N` proxies for deterministic sequence models (`N=40000`, `k<=800`).

Data file:
- `data/ep987_exponential_sum_models.json`

Model behavior:
- arithmetic `x_j={j alpha}` with `alpha=sqrt(2)-1`: very small `A_k/k` tail.
- van der Corput base-2: sublinear but larger spikes.
- quadratic `x_j={alpha j^2}`: much larger finite spikes.

## Interpretation
Finite model behavior is diverse and supports that growth strongly depends on structure of the
sequence. This does not settle the asymptotic existence question.
