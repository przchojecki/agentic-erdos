### Proposition BK1 (Ramsey equivalence)
\(R(3,k)>n\) iff there exists a triangle-free graph \(G\) on \(n\) vertices with
\(\alpha(G)<k\).

\textit{Proof.}

### Proposition BK2 (Constant extraction reduction)
If one proves constants \(c_-,c_+\) with
\[
\alpha(G)\ge (c_-+o(1))\sqrt{n\log n}
\]
for all triangle-free \(G\), and constructions with
\[
\alpha(G)\le (c_++o(1))\sqrt{n\log n},
\]
then
\[
\frac{1}{c_+^2}\le \liminf \frac{R(3,k)\log k}{k^2}
\le \limsup \frac{R(3,k)\log k}{k^2}\le \frac{1}{c_-^2}.
\]

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Apply BK1 and invert \(k\asymp \sqrt{n\log n}\).
\(\square\)
