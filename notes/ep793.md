# EP-793 deeper attempt

## Statement
Let `F(n)` be the largest `|A|` with `A subset [1,n]` such that no `a in A` divides the
product of two other distinct elements of `A`.

## Exact finite result proved in this attempt
I reduced the condition to a 3-uniform hypergraph independence problem and solved it exactly
for `6<=n<=50`.

Data file:
- `data/ep793_exact_small_hypergraph_scan.json`

Sample exact values:
- `F(30)=11`
- `F(50)=16`

## Interpretation
This is a rigorous finite table, not an asymptotic proof of
`F(n)=pi(n)+(C+o(1)) n^(2/3)/(log n)^2`.
