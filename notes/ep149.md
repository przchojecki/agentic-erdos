## LaTeX Proofs for A => B => C Skeleton

### Proposition AZ1 (Equivalent formulation)
For graph \(G\), the strong chromatic index \(\chi'_s(G)\) equals
\[
\chi\!\left(L(G)^2\right).
\]

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Two edges require distinct strong colors iff they are adjacent or distance \(1\)
in \(G\), equivalently adjacent in \(L(G)^2\).
\(\square\)

### Proposition AZ2 (Reduction to sparse-coloring bound)
If one proves
\[
\chi(H)\le \frac54\Delta^2+o(\Delta^2)
\]
for \(H=L(G)^2\) using its structural sparsity constraints, then the conjectural
asymptotic upper bound for \(\chi'_s(G)\) follows by AZ1.

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Substitute \(H=L(G)^2\) into AZ1.
\(\square\)
