# EP-1065

## Problem Statement
Study infinitude of primes in forms
\[
p=2^a q+1
\]
and
\[
p=2^a3^b q+1,
\]
with $q$ prime.

## Literature
- Problem remains open for infinitude in both forms.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Replaced stub with standalone large-range prime scan.
- Deepened limit to
\[
22{,}000{,}000
\]
and tracked densities at multiple large probes.

## Computation-Guided Observations
- Both forms occur frequently in finite ranges scanned.
- Observed prime-relative densities decrease gradually with range in this run (still clearly positive at the largest probe).
- Finite positivity is compatible with infinitude heuristics but not a proof.
