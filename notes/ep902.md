# EP-902 deeper attempt with exhaustive/random search

## Statement
`f(n)`: smallest tournament size such that every `n`-set is dominated by some outside
vertex.

## Exact check in this attempt
I exhaustively verified the `n=2` boundary:
- no tournament on `m=6` has the property,
- at least one tournament on `m=7` has it.

Data file:
- `data/ep902_exhaustive_n2.json`

This matches known `f(2)=7`.

## Additional random search
`data/ep902_random_tournament_search_extended.json` shows very low hit rates for small
`m` and no hits in sampled ranges for `n=3,4`, consistent with rapid growth.

## Hard point
Sharp asymptotics between known lower/upper bounds remain open.
