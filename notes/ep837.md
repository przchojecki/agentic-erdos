# EP-837

## Problem Statement
For density-jump sets $A_k$ in $k$-uniform hypergraphs, determine $A_3$.

## Literature
- Erdos-Simonovits problem.
- The $k=2$ case is known completely:
  $$A_2=\left\{1-\frac1m: m\ge 1\right\}.$$
- For $k=3$, a full explicit characterization is not resolved in this repository.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Ran a heuristic finite probe for jump-like behavior in random 3-uniform hypergraphs.
- For each target global density $p$, sampled random 3-graphs on $n=80$.
- Measured sampled minimum induced-subgraph density on 18-vertex subsets and compared to global density.

## Computation-Guided Observations
- Across $p\in[0.05,0.3]$, local minimum sampled densities are systematically below global density.
- Average global-minus-local gap observed around $0.02$-$0.05$ in these runs.
- This is only exploratory random-model evidence and does not characterize $A_3$.
