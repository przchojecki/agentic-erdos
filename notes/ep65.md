## LaTeX Proofs for A => B => C Skeleton

### Proposition R1 (Stability implies asymptotic optimality)
If every near-minimizer is \(o(k)\)-edit-close to template \(T_k\), and
\[
\sum_{a\in T_k}\frac1a = \left(\frac12+o(1)\right)\log k,
\]
then the extremal minimum has the same asymptotic.

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
By closeness, reciprocal sums differ by \(o(\log k)\) under the stability
penalty bound; combine with template value.
\(\square\)

### Proposition R2 (Uniqueness up to \(o(k)\)-edits)
Under strict stability penalty, any two minimizers differ by \(o(k)\) elements.

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
If not, penalty would force one to have strictly larger objective than minimum.
\(\square\)
