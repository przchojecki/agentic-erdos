# EP-396 deep attempt with standalone computation

## Statement
For each fixed `k`, does there exist `n` such that
`\prod_{0\le i\le k}(n-i)\mid\binom{2n}{n}`?

## What is resolved from background
- One-shift divisibility (`n-k\mid\binom{2n}{n}` for fixed `k`) has strong
  positive results.
- The full descending-product divisibility remains open.

## Attempt route
Computed exact prime-valuation divisibility profiles for
`\binom{2n}{n}` against descending products.
