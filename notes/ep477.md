# EP-477 deep attempt (no partial theorem extracted)

## Statement
Does there exist a degree-`>=2` polynomial `f:Z->Z` and set `A subset Z` such
that every integer has a unique representation `n=a+b` with `a in A` and
`b in f(Z)`?

for polynomial image sets.
