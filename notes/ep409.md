# EP-409 deep attempt with standalone computation

## Statement
For `T(n)=\phi(n)+1`, how many iterations are needed to hit a prime? Can
infinitely many `n` hit the same prime? What is the density of integers reaching
a fixed prime?

## Attempt route
Computed exact iteration depths and terminal-prime basins on a large finite
range using memoized dynamics.

## New Experiments
- 2026-03-12T09:49:51.609Z: standalone deep profile (`scripts/ep409.mjs`) up to
  `n<=10^6`.

- Terminal-prime basin sizes are highly nonuniform; several primes attract

## Status
