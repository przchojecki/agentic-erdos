# EP-564

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

### Source: ep564_partial.md

## Statement
For 2-color 3-uniform Ramsey numbers `R_3(n)`, ask whether there exists `c>0`
with
\[
R_3(n)\ge 2^{2^{cn}}.
\]

## Literature Leads
- Erdős-Hajnal-Rado (1965): classical bounds `2^{c n^2} < R_3(n) < 2^{2^n}`.
- Erdős-Hajnal-Máté-Rado (1984): doubly-exponential lower bound in related
  multi-color setting.
- Problem page (last edited January 18, 2026) records this as open and as a
  special case of EP-562.

## Strategy Notes
- Transfer pathways from multi-color lower bounds to 2-color remain a plausible
  route.
- Another route is sharpening stepping-up lower constructions while controlling
