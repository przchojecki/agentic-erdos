# EP-741 partial attempt

## Route
Tested finite random models where `A+A` is dense and searched over many random bipartitions `A=A_1 \sqcup A_2` to maximize `min(d(A_1+A_1), d(A_2+A_2))`.

## Evidence from this batch
- `data/ep741_partition_density_random.json` (`N=1200`, 30 instances per density, 500 partitions each):
  - in every tested instance, both parts had positive finite-window sumset density under some partition.
  - average best minimum densities ranged from `0.50` (at `p=0.10`) up to `0.94` (at `p=0.30`).

## Hard point
Finite random evidence does not control adversarial sparse-structure sets with positive asymptotic `A+A` density.

## Status
Heuristic positive signal; general proof remains open.
