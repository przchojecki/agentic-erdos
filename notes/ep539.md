# EP-539

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

### Source: ep539_partial.md

## Statement
For
\[
h(n)=\min_{|A|=n}\left|\left\{\frac{a}{(a,b)}:a,b\in A\right\}\right|,
\]
determine the true growth rate.

## What is resolved from background
Known two-sided bounds place `h(n)` in a broad window between roughly
`n^{1/2}` and `n^{1-c}`.

## What remains open
Exact exponent/asymptotic behavior is unresolved.

## New Experiments
- 2026-03-12T11:07:55.801Z: standalone deep search (`scripts/ep539.mjs`) over
  random and structured candidate sets.
- Best sets in sampled ranges were heavily smooth/divisor-structured and gave
  significantly smaller ratio-set size than random sets.
- Finite best values increased with `n`, while staying far below random-sample
  means in each tested configuration.
