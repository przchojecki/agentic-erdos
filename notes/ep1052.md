# EP-1052

## Problem Statement
Search for unitary perfect numbers, i.e. integers $n$ with
\[
\sigma^*(n)=2n,
\]
where $\sigma^*$ is the sum of unitary divisors.

## Literature
- Classical list of known examples is very short; odd unitary-perfect examples are unknown.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Added standalone exact scan using SPF-based factorization and multiplicative formula
\[
\sigma^*(n)=\prod_{p^a\parallel n}(p^a+1).
\]
- Deepened range to
\[
n\le 20{,}000{,}000.
\]

## Computation-Guided Observations
- Hits up to this bound are exactly
\[
6,\ 60,\ 90,\ 87360.
\]
- No odd hits were found.

## Status
- Global finiteness/infinitude questions remain open.
- Added deeper standalone exact-range verification.
