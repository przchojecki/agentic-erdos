# EP-533

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

### Source: ep533_partial.md

## Statement
Dense `K_5`-free graphs should contain a triangle-free induced subset of linear
size `c(\delta)n`.

## What is known from background
- Proven in the stronger density regime `\delta > 1/16`.
- `K_4`-free analogue is known for all `\delta>0`.
- Crossing the full `K_5`-free threshold remains open.

## New Experiments
- 2026-03-12T11:04:31.060Z: standalone deep finite search
  (`scripts/ep533.mjs`).
- Generated dense random `K_5`-free graphs by edge-addition with explicit `K_5`
  checks, then searched for large induced triangle-free subsets via many
  randomized restarts.
- Best found subset ratios in tested sizes:
  - `n=40`: `16` (`0.40n`),
  - `n=50`: `18` (`0.36n`),
  - `n=60`: `22` (`0.367n`).
