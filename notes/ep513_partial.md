# EP-513 deeper attempt

## Statement
For transcendental entire `f(z)=sum a_n z^n`, maximize
`liminf_{r->infinity} max_n |a_n r^n| / M(r,f)`.

## What is known
Background places the value in a strict interval around `1/2`:
- trivial lower range starts at `1/2`,
- unpublished observation gives `>1/2`,
- Clunie-Hayman gives a strict upper bound below `2/pi`.

## Hard point
No exact extremal value/construction is known from these bounds.
