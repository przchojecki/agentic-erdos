## Statement
For lacunary entire `f(z)=\sum a_k z^{n_k}`, does `n_k/k\to\infty` force every
value to be taken infinitely often?

## What is resolved
Background includes strong sufficient conditions (e.g. `\sum 1/n_k<\infty`) and
finite-order gap theorems, but not the full criterion.
