## LaTeX Proofs for A => B => C Skeleton

### Proposition BN1 (Classical counting lower bound)
If \(m\) marks cover all differences \(1,\dots,N\), then
\[
\binom{m}{2}\ge N,
\]
hence \(m\ge \sqrt{2N}+O(1)\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Each unordered pair contributes one difference; at most \(\binom{m}{2}\)
distinct positive differences are available.
\(\square\)

### Proposition BN2 (Constant-extraction reduction)
If one proves constructions with \(m\le (c_++o(1))\sqrt N\) and universal lower
bound \(m\ge (c_- -o(1))\sqrt N\), then asymptotic constant lies in
\([c_-,c_+]\); matching \(c_-=c_+\) resolves EP-170.

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Divide bounds by \(\sqrt N\) and pass to limit superior/inferior.
\(\square\)
