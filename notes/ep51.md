## LaTeX Proofs for A => B => C Skeleton

### Proposition M1 (Envelope implication)
If \(L(a)\to\infty\), then \(R(a)\to\infty\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Since \(R(a)\ge L(a)\) for all large \(a\), divergence of \(L\) forces
divergence of \(R\).
\(\square\)

### Proposition M2 (Subsequence boundedness implication)
If there exists \(a_j\to\infty\) with \(R(a_j)\le C\), then
\[
\liminf_{a\to\infty}R(a)\le C.
\]

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
By definition of liminf along a subsequence.
\(\square\)
