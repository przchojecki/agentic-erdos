## Lemma Chain (with Proofs)

### Lemma 1 (Submultiplicative lower-envelope reduction)
If \(S\) satisfies
\[
S(N+M)\ge S(N)S(M),
\]
then \(\log S(N)\) is superadditive.

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Take logarithms of both sides.
\(\square\)

### Lemma 2 (Asymptotic extraction from additive scale)
If
\[
c_1\frac{N}{\log N}\le \log S(N)\le c_2\frac{N}{\log N}
\]
for large \(N\), then \(S(N)=\exp(\Theta(N/\log N))\).

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Exponentiate both inequalities.
\(\square\)

### Lemma 3 (Constant identification reduction)
If one proves
\[
\liminf \frac{\log S(N)\log N}{N}\ge c,\qquad
\limsup \frac{\log S(N)\log N}{N}\le c,
\]
then
\[
\log S(N)\sim c\frac{N}{\log N}.
\]

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Matching liminf/limsup yields convergence to \(c\).
\(\square\)
