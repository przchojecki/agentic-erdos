## Statement
For tree `T` on `n` vertices and complete multipartite `G` with class sizes
`m_1\le\cdots\le m_k`, show (for large `n`) a bound of shape
\[
R(T,G) \le (\chi(G)-1)(R(T,K_{m_1,m_2})-1)+m_1.
\]

## What is resolved from background
- Base reduction `k=2` is immediate.
- Clique/tree exact formulas are known in related cases, but do not directly
  prove this multipartite transfer.
