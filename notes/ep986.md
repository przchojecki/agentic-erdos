# EP-986

## Problem Statement
For fixed $k$ and large $t$, estimate off-diagonal Ramsey growth, especially lower bounds.
For $k=3$, this corresponds to constructing triangle-free graphs with small independence number.

## Literature
- For $k=3$, asymptotic order is known: $R(3,t)=\Theta(t^2/\log t)$.
- General fixed-$k$ lower-bound landscape remains broader.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Added standalone deeper constructive scan for $k=3$ witnesses:
  - random triangle-free process on sizes $n=24,\dots,64$,
  - exact independence number computation per sampled graph,
  - best-of-many-trials witness extraction per $n$.

## Computation-Guided Observations
- Best witnesses in this run include:
  - $n=48$: $\alpha(G)=14$, giving $R(3,15)>48$;
  - $n=64$: $\alpha(G)=17$, giving $R(3,18)>64$.
- Edge densities in best witnesses are stable around $0.11$-$0.15$ across tested sizes.
- Finite profile is consistent with expected triangle-free low-independence scaling regime.

## Status
- General statements remain open.
- Added deeper standalone exact-witness computation across larger $n$ and many trials.
