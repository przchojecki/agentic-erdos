## LaTeX Proofs for A => B => C Skeleton

### Proposition AU1 (Monotonicity and submultiplicativity template)
For fixed \(k\), \(r_k(N)\) is nondecreasing and satisfies (up to constants)
\[
r_k(MN)\ge r_k(M)\,r_k(N).
\]

\[
\textit{Proof sketch.}
\]
Take product-type constructions on digit expansions/base decomposition; AP-free
property is preserved under the standard combinatorial product embedding.
\(\square\)

### Proposition AU2 (Exponent-gap reduction)
If
\[
N^{\alpha_k-o(1)}\le r_k(N)\le N^{\beta_k+o(1)},
\]
then improving either \(\alpha_k\) upward or \(\beta_k\) downward directly narrows
the asymptotic uncertainty interval.

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Normalize by \(\log N\): \(\log r_k(N)/\log N\in[\alpha_k-o(1),\beta_k+o(1)]\).
\(\square\)
