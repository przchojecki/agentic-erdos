# EP-627 deeper attempt

## Statement
Let `f(n)=max_G chi(G)/omega(G)` over `|V(G)|=n`. Ask whether
`f(n)/(n/(log n)^2)` has a limit.

## What is known
Background gives
`f(n) ~ n/(log n)^2`
up to multiplicative constants, and a restricted interval where the limit would lie if
it exists.

## Hard point
No method here proves convergence of the normalized ratio; only two-sided asymptotics
are currently cited.
