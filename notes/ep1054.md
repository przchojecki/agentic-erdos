# EP-1054

## Problem Statement
For each $n$, consider minimal $m$ such that $n$ is a prefix sum of the sorted divisors of $m$; study growth of this minimal witness function.

## Literature
- Strongest $o(n)$ formulation is known to fail; refined growth behavior remains open.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Added standalone bounded exact search with SPF divisor generation.
- Deepened bounds to
\[
N_{\text{target}}=4700,\qquad M_{\max}=220000.
\]

## Computation-Guided Observations
- Unresolved values in this bounded box remain exactly
\[
\{2,5\}.
\]
- Many probe values have witnesses with $m/n$ near $1$; finite maximum ratio in scanned range was about $8.7241$.
- This is bounded evidence only and does not settle asymptotic growth.
