## Route
Reviewed degree constraints in `4`-critical graphs and compared known constructions against the target linear minimum degree.

## What is resolved from background

- The open target asks for arbitrarily large `n` with minimum degree `>> n`.

## Hard point
Existing `4`-critical constructions remain far from linear minimum degree, and no obstruction/proof principle currently closes that gap.
