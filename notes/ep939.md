# EP-939

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

### Working statement
Search for pairwise coprime additive patterns among \(r\)-powerful numbers:
\[
a+b=c\quad (r=3,4),\qquad a+b+c=d\quad (r=5).
\]

### Deep standalone computation (`ep939.mjs`)
- Method: exact SPF scan up to \(B\), classify by minimum prime-adic exponent, then brute-force additive tests with pairwise-coprime filters.
- Run: \(B=5\cdot 10^7\).
- Counts of \(r\)-powerful numbers up to \(B\):
  - \(r=3\): 1281
  - \(r=4\): 380
  - \(r=5\): 177
- Found patterns:
  - coprime \(r=3\), \(a+b=c\): 0
  - coprime \(r=4\), \(a+b=c\): 0
  - coprime \(r=5\), \(a+b+c=d\): 0

### Interpretation
No small-height examples appear even with a much larger bound, which is consistent with rarity/structured constructions rather than random density.
