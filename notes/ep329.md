## Deeper Proof Program (A => B => C)

### Target
Determine the exact constant in
\[
\limsup_{N\to\infty}\frac{F(N)}{\sqrt N},
\]
where \(F(N)\) is maximal Sidon subset size of \([1,N]\).

### Step A: Construction side sharpening
Transfer finite-field constructions to intervals with minimal loss.

### Step B: Upper-bound side sharpening

### Step C: Constant matching
Close the gap between lower and upper constants.

sets in cyclic groups to \([1,N]\).

## Lemma Chain (with Proofs)

### Lemma 1 (Difference counting upper bound)
If \(A\subset[1,N]\) is Sidon and \(|A|=m\), then
\[
\binom m2 \le N-1.
\]

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
All positive differences \(a-b\) (\(a>b\), \(a,b\in A\)) are distinct in a Sidon
set. These differences lie in \(\{1,\dots,N-1\}\), giving at most \(N-1\) values.
\(\square\)

### Lemma 2 (Classical asymptotic upper estimate)
From Lemma 1,
\[
m\le \frac{1+\sqrt{1+8(N-1)}}{2}=\sqrt{2N}+O(1).
\]

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Solve quadratic inequality \(m(m-1)\le 2(N-1)\).
\(\square\)

### Lemma 3 (Constant extraction reduction)
If one proves lower constructions with
\[
F(N)\ge c_1\sqrt N-o(\sqrt N)
\]
and upper bound \(F(N)\le c_2\sqrt N+o(\sqrt N)\), then limsup constant lies in
\([c_1,c_2]\); matching \(c_1=c_2\) resolves EP-329.

\[
\textit{Proof.}
\]
Divide by \(\sqrt N\), take limsup/liminf bounds.
\(\square\)
