# EP-700

## Problem Statement
Define
\[
f(n)=\min_{1<k\le n/2} \gcd\!\left(n,\binom{n}{k}\right).
\]
For composite \(n\), determine structure/size of \(f(n)\), including equality patterns with \(n/P(n)\) (\(P(n)\): largest prime factor).

## Literature
- Known background includes:
  - general upper bound \(f(n)\le n/P(n)\),
  - equality in notable families (including semiprimes and specific examples),
  - infinitely many composite \(n\) with \(f(n)>\sqrt n\).
- Full characterization remains open.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Exact standalone scan for all composite \(n\le N\):
  - factored each \(n\),
  - computed \(p\)-adic valuations of \(\binom{n}{k}\) for each \(k\),
  - evaluated the exact minimum gcd value \(f(n)\).
- Tracked frequencies of equality \(f(n)=n/P(n)\) and threshold exceedance \(f(n)>\sqrt n\).

## Computation-Guided Observations
- Deep run to \(N=12000\) (10561 composite integers scanned).
- At \(n\le 12000\):
  - fraction with \(f(n)=n/P(n)\) about \(0.5799\),
  - fraction with \(f(n)>\sqrt n\) about \(0.0471\).
- Maximum observed ratio \(f(n)/(n/P(n))\) remained 1 in this range (consistent with the upper bound).
