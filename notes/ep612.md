# EP-612

## Problem Statement
Diameter-versus-degree extremal behavior in \(K_4\)-free (and related forbidden-subgraph) graphs: sharpen constants in bounds of the form \(D \lesssim C \cdot n/d\).

## Literature / Context
- Prior notes indicate original conjectural forms are false in some branches and amended constants remain open.
- The key question is sharp constant behavior in constrained families.

## Our Approaches / What Is Proven
- Implemented deeper explicit-family computations on triangle-free odd-cycle blowups.
- Measured coefficient \((D d)/n\) and compared against historical and amended benchmark constants.

## Computation-Guided Observations
- For tested blowup families, \((Dd)/n\) lies around \(0.889\) to \(0.952\), increasing with cycle length.
- These values remain far below larger conjectural upper constants in this branch.

## Next Proof Targets
- Add non-blowup constructions to test whether coefficient can be pushed substantially higher.
- Convert explicit-family observations into general upper/lower comparison lemmas.
