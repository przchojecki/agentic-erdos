# EP-538

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

### Source: ep538_partial.md

## Statement
Under a representation-multiplicity cap on `m=pa` (`p` prime, `a\in A`), bound
`\sum_{n\in A} 1/n` as sharply as possible.

## What is resolved from background
A general upper bound of shape
\[
\sum_{n\in A}\frac{1}{n} \ll r\,\frac{\log N}{\log\log N}
\]
is known by double counting.

## What remains open
Sharp dependence on `r` and `N` is still open.
