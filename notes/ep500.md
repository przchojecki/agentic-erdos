# EP-500

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

## Statement
Determine `\operatorname{ex}_3(n,K_4^3)` asymptotically (Tur\'an density form of
`K_4^3`-free 3-graphs).

## What is known from background
Classical balanced Tur\'an-type constructions give lower bounds near `5/9`, while
Razborov-type methods give upper bounds above that value; the exact asymptotic
constant remains open.

## New Experiments

  (`scripts/ep500.mjs`) with heavy trials (`TRIAL_SCALE=20`).
- Best densities found:
  - `n=20`: `0.47807018` (24,000 trials),
  - `n=24`: `0.45355731` (18,000 trials),
  - `n=28`: `0.43345543` (14,000 trials),
  - `n=32`: `0.41713710` (10,000 trials).
- In all tested `n`, random greedy remains far below balanced Tur\'an
  construction densities (`~0.577` to `~0.589`).
