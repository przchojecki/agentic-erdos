# EP-1131 partial attempt

## Route
Used interpolation-kernel reformulation and compared known two-sided asymptotic bounds for `min I(x_1,...,x_n)`.

## What is resolved from background
- Known bounds:
  - `2 - O((log n)^2/n) <= min I`
  - `min I <= 2 - 2/(2n-1)`
- Thus the minimum is known to approach `2` from below at roughly `1/n` scale up to logarithmic slack.

## Hard point
Determining the exact first-order constant in the `1/n` term (the conjectural `2-(1+o(1))/n`) remains open.

## Status
Partially resolved with sharp-scale bounds, not fully closed.
