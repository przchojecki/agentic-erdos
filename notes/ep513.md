# EP-513

## Proof Attempts and Literature Notes

### Source: ep513_partial.md

## Statement
For transcendental entire `f(z)=\sum a_n z^n`, maximize
\[
\liminf_{r\to\infty}\frac{\max_n |a_n|r^n}{M(r,f)}.
\]

## What is known
Background places the value in a strict interval around `1/2`:
- lower side starts at `1/2` and is known to be strictly larger,
- Clunie-Hayman gives a strict upper bound below `2/\pi`.
